Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Access

Q: How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. An OTP will be sent to your registered email. OTPs are valid for 15 minutes. After 5 failed attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes.

Q: Can I have multiple users under one company account?

Yes. The primary admin can invite unlimited users. Each user gets individual credentials. Roles available: Admin, Manager, Viewer, API-only.

Orders & Payments

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, UPI (India), Net Banking, and NEFT/RTGS for enterprise invoicing. Cryptocurrency is not supported.

Q: How do I get a refund?

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase via the Support portal. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Subscription fees are non-refundable after 7 days.

Technical & API

Q: What is the API rate limit?

Standard plan: 1,000 requests/hour. Enterprise plan: 10,000 requests/hour. Rate limit headers are returned on every response: X-RateLimit-Limit, X-RateLimit-Remaining, X-RateLimit-Reset

Q: Is there an SLA for API uptime?

Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% uptime SLA with credits for downtime exceeding the threshold. Check status.example.com for live status.

Security & Compliance

Q: Is the platform GDPR compliant?

Yes. We are GDPR, SOC 2 Type II, and ISO 27001 certified. Data is stored in EU data centres for European customers. DPA agreements are available on request from legal@example.com.
