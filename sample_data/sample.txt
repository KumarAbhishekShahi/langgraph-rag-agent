ENTERPRISE PLATFORM — PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT
=====================================================
Version: 2.1  |  Last Updated: 2026-03-01  |  Owner: Product Team

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
This document describes the requirements for the Enterprise Customer Portal v3.0.
The portal serves 50,000+ business customers across APAC and Europe.

KEY MODULES
-----------
1. Authentication & Identity
   - OAuth2/OIDC with Azure AD and Okta
   - Multi-Factor Authentication (TOTP + push)
   - Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with 12 permission levels
   - Session timeout: 8 hours idle / 24 hours absolute

2. Order Management
   - Real-time order tracking with GPS integration
   - Bulk order import via CSV (up to 10,000 rows)
   - Order status webhooks to customer ERP systems
   - SLA: Order confirmation within 500ms

3. Billing & Invoicing
   - Automated invoice generation on order completion
   - Multi-currency support (USD, EUR, INR, SGD)
   - GST/VAT calculation by customer region
   - Payment gateway: Stripe (global), Razorpay (India)

4. Reporting & Analytics
   - Real-time dashboards (< 2s refresh)
   - Scheduled reports via email (daily/weekly/monthly)
   - Data export: CSV, Excel, PDF
   - Retention: 7 years for financial reports

NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
- Availability: 99.9% uptime SLA
- Response Time: p95 < 200ms for all API calls
- Scalability: Handle 10x traffic spikes (auto-scaling)
- Security: ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II compliant
- GDPR: Right to erasure within 30 days of request
